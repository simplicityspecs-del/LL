Refund Policy.

Premium access is a digital subscription. This policy explains how cancellations and refund requests are handled.

Cancellations

You can cancel future renewals through the account billing portal. Cancelling stops future billing but does not automatically refund the current paid period.

Refund Requests

Refunds are reviewed case by case. We generally do not refund a completed billing period after premium content or lock alerts have been accessed, unless required by law or there has been a duplicate charge or clear billing error.

Billing Errors

If you believe you were charged incorrectly, contact us as soon as possible with the email used at checkout and the Stripe receipt details.

How To Contact Us

Send refund questions to support@livepicks.app.
