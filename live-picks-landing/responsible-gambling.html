Responsible Gambling.

Live Picks is analysis only. Gambling can cause financial and personal harm. Do not use this service if betting is affecting your wellbeing.

Important Warning

Chances are you are about to lose. No model, read, alert or analyst can guarantee a betting outcome.

Use Limits

  • Only bet money you can afford to lose.
  • Never chase losses.
  • Do not borrow money to gamble.
  • Take breaks and avoid betting when angry, tired or under pressure.
  • Use deposit limits, time-outs or self-exclusion tools where available.
