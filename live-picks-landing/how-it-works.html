How it works

Pending reads, locked picks and fight-night alerts.

Live Picks MMA keeps the premium feed simple: each UFC fight is either pending or locked. Pending means no official pick yet. Locked means the official H2H read is live.

Subscriber flow Open feed. Watch status. Get lock alert.

The page is built for fight night, not a maze of betting slips and prop markets.

Simple process

The live feed has three states.

Before cardEvent boardFights and odds are loaded before the event window.
During cardPending readsThe matchup is being watched but no official pick has been released.
LockOfficial pickThe premium side, notes and alert are sent when the read is live.

Why H2H only?

Round and method markets can be noisy, thin and heavily bookmaker-dependent. Live Picks MMA avoids that spread. The product stays focused on whether one fighter is mispriced against another in the head-to-head market.

That makes the feed easier to read and the record easier to judge.

How alerts work.

  • Push notifications: subscribers can enable browser/app-style notifications for lock alerts.
  • Email backup: a lock email is also sent as a failsafe so the pick is not relying on one alert channel.
  • Premium feed: the feed remains the source of truth for current event status, notes and lock state.
When do picks lock?

There is no fixed minute. A pick locks only when the model and fight-night read are ready.

Are all fights picked?

No. Some fights may stay pending or pass if the read is not strong enough.
