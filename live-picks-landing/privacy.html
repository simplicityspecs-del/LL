Privacy Policy.

This explains what we collect, why we collect it, and how subscriber data is handled.

Information We Collect

We collect information you submit, including email address, password credentials handled through Supabase, newsletter signup source, account requests, and support messages.

Payments

Payments are processed by Stripe. We do not store full card numbers. Stripe may provide us subscription IDs, customer IDs, payment status, billing status and renewal dates.

Push Notifications

If you enable push alerts, we store the browser push subscription needed to send lock notifications to that device. You can disable notifications in your browser or device settings.

How We Use Data

We use data to provide premium access, send newsletter or lock alerts, manage billing status, respond to support requests, prevent abuse and maintain service security.

Service Providers

Live Picks uses providers such as Stripe, Supabase, Resend and Vercel to operate payments, accounts, email, hosting and data storage.

Your Choices

You can request account help, email updates, correction or deletion by contacting support@livepicks.app. Some records may be retained where required for billing, legal or security purposes.
